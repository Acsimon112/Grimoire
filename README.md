The be all end all notetaking app for your dungeons & dragons 5th edition gaming needs!

## Features:
### 1. Create and Manage Notes!
Create notes, edit them with a powerful rich text editor and make your plans look 
prettier than ever before. Using flutter quill, your documents may be formatted 
to your hearts' content.
### 2. Share Your Notes!
Share notes with your players through a QR code generated by the app. Simply open 
a document in the editor, open the sidebar, and select "Share" to generate a code! 
Then, from the home page, the recipient can select "Scan Code" to view the document.
### 3. Roll dice!
Grimoire comes built-in with a dice roller for easy rolling straight from your phone!
